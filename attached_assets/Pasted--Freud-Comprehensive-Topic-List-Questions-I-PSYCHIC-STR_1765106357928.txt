# Freud: Comprehensive Topic List & Questions

## I. PSYCHIC STRUCTURE & TOPOGRAPHY

**Topics:**
- The unconscious, preconscious, and conscious systems
- The id, ego, and superego (structural model)
- Primary vs. secondary process thinking
- The pleasure principle vs. the reality principle
- Psychic energy (libido) and its economics
- Cathexis and countercathexis
- The censorship function

**Questions:**
1. What is the relationship between the unconscious and the id—are they coextensive?
2. How does the ego mediate between the demands of the id, superego, and external reality?
3. What distinguishes primary process thinking from secondary process thinking?
4. How does the reality principle develop out of the pleasure principle?
5. What is the nature of psychic energy—is it quantifiable?
6. How does repression maintain the boundary between conscious and unconscious?
7. What is the superego's relationship to the father complex?

**Questions:**

6. What is the relationship between the compulsion to repeat and the death drive?
7. Can drives ever be fully satisfied, or is tension constitutive of psychic life?
